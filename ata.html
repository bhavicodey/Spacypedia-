About the Author

Bhavishyaa is a space and science enthusiast. She loves stargazing and coding, her goal is to produce space knowledge to everyone, She wanted to make a way for anyone to access space info and teach people about space. So she created this website using HTML & CSS. She loves going to sessions on space and coding and that's how she got the inspiration of making this website.




  • "I have loved coding every since I was 7 years old. My mom and dad bought me this robot ball, and I used my IPAD to change it's colors and make it move.",


    Her parents wanted to see if she liked coding. "We came across this software called "Scratch", and I started researching on it". Soon she became a certified scratch coder, then this pandemic came over. "I wanted to find a way to make a contribution to the community."

    Bhavi decided to make a coding camp online to teach kids about coding, "We held a zoom session and everyday we had a 1-hour session to learn about scratch"

    Later she started doing sessions for other oragizations too. Like CAGIS (Canadian Association for Girls in Science) and Unstructured Studio.
