Astronomer

This is what I want to be when I grow up too! :)

Astronomers are scientists who study the universe, its objects and how it works. They aim to push the boundaries of human knowledge about how the universe works by observation and theoretical modelling. Salary: The median annual wage for astronomers is $114,590. The median annual wage for physicists is $122,850. Job Outlook: Overall employment of physicists and astronomers is projected to grow 7 percent over the next ten years, faster than the average for all occupations.

Astrobiology, formerly known as exobiology, is an interdisciplinary scientific field concerned with the origins, early evolution, distribution, and future of life in the universe. Astrobiology considers the question of whether extraterrestrial life exists, and if it does, how humans can detect it.
